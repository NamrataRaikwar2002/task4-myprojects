Delhi:Heart of india

Delhi is a city and national capita territory (NCT), in the north-central of India. It is one of the seven union territories of India.And also known as the heart of India. It is situated on the west bank of the Yamuna river. It is famous for many things, many historical places like red fort, Qutub Minar, Sansad Bhavan, food, temple, Delhi Universities, IIT(Indian Insitute Of Technology).

The population of Delhi is 1.9 crores.

India Gate

As India gate is the most visited place in Delhi. It is located in Rajpath in Delhi. it built in 1931 people come here from many places to see the beauty of the India gate, how nicely it is erected. The Amar jawan Jyoti lighted for 24/7 hours, the night scene is really very awesome here many lights with moonshine make it awesome. it is also known as the all India war memorial. It is a memorial in honor of many soldiers who lose their lives in the first world war. Amar jawan Jyoti inaugurated by Indira Gandhi when she the pm of India, in honor of the soldiers who sacrificed their lives in the Indo-Pakistan war in 1972.
